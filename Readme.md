# 📊 Customer Churn Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview
Customer churn is one of the major challenges faced by telecom companies. This project analyzes customer churn behavior using **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)** and visualization techniques to identify the key factors affecting customer retention.

The project provides business insights and recommendations that can help telecom companies reduce customer churn and improve customer satisfaction.

---

# 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze customer churn patterns
- Identify high-risk customer segments
- Understand factors influencing churn
- Generate business insights for retention strategies
- Visualize customer behavior using charts and graphs

# 🔍 Key Business Insights

## 📉 Overall Churn Rate
- Overall churn rate: **26.54%**
- Approximately **1 out of every 4 customers** leaves the service

---

## ⏳ Customer Tenure Analysis
- Customers with **0–2 months tenure** show highest churn (~45–50%)
- Customers with tenure greater than **24 months** show lower churn (<15%)

### Insight:
Early-stage customers are more likely to leave the service.

---

## 📄 Contract Type Analysis

| Contract Type | Churn Rate |
|---|---|
| Month-to-Month | ~40–45% |
| One-Year | ~10–15% |
| Two-Year | <5% |

### Insight:
Long-term contracts significantly improve customer retention.

---

## 🛡️ Value-Added Services Analysis

Customers without:
- Online Security
- Tech Support
- Device Protection

show higher churn (~35–45%).

Customers using these services show lower churn (~15–20%).

### Insight:
Bundling value-added services can reduce churn.

---

## 🌐 Internet Service Analysis

| Internet Type | Churn Rate |
|---|---|
| Fiber Optic | ~40% |
| DSL | ~20% |

### Insight:
Fiber Optic users experience higher churn, possibly due to pricing or service quality issues.

---

## 💳 Payment Method Analysis

| Payment Method | Churn Rate |
|---|---|
| Electronic Check | ~45% |
| Auto-Pay Methods | ~15–20% |

### Insight:
Automated payment methods improve retention.

---

## 💰 Monthly Charges Analysis
- Higher monthly charges (>70) lead to higher churn (~35–40%)
- Lower monthly charges reduce churn (~15–20%)

### Insight:
Customers are highly price-sensitive.

---

## 👴 Demographic Analysis
- Senior citizens show higher churn (~40%)
- Non-senior customers show lower churn (~25%)

### Insight:
Senior citizens may require better customer support and engagement.

---

# 💡 Business Recommendations

✅ Improve onboarding experience for new customers  
✅ Promote long-term contract plans  
✅ Encourage adoption of additional services  
✅ Improve Fiber Optic service quality  
✅ Optimize pricing strategies  
✅ Promote auto-payment methods  
✅ Provide personalized support for senior citizens  

---

# 📌 Conclusion
Customer churn is mainly driven by:
- Short-term contracts
- Lack of service engagement
- High pricing
- Poor customer experience

By implementing targeted retention strategies, telecom companies can significantly reduce churn and improve customer loyalty.
